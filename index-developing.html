Innovative design hub based on the border between
Tijuana, Mex. - San Diego CA.

Through a comprehensive design process, we develop spaces that increase the value of your investment by integrating scalable design strategies

The Challenge

Working between cities, governments, community developers, investors and homeowners to create increase structures that capitalize legislation and building codes to improve communities. Navigating the Tijuana-San Diego border's dual identity requires innovative architectural solutions that bridge cultural and economic divides. The task: design affordable spaces that harmonize with the region's urban narratives while promoting social equity. Al Border Arquitectos, we transform these challenges into opportunities, ensuring our projects resonate with the unique fabric of this frontier region.

The Approach

At Border Arquitectos, our methodology starts with immersive observation and exploration with technology. Integrating Building information Modeling into our workflow. BIM enhances our ability to create detailed digital representations of our projects. improving coordination. visualization, and simulation from design through construction. This approach fosters closer collaboration with clients and partners, ensuring our designs meet their visions and requirements while facilitating innovation in architecture and urbanism.
